Our Venue

Our class was held at Work Nicer co-working space in downtown Calgary. We have partnered with them in order to offer you the use of their beautiful space as well as show you where you can work when you take off. They recognize the exceptional talent in our students just as we do, making them more than willing to network with our students. Our students have the unique opportunity to be exposed to the Work Nicer environment where multiple startups work in one space, providing an opportunity to network and build off each other's momentum. Work Nicer is a high tech, modern and friendly place for startups to learn from other startups in their early stages of progress. We’ve hand selected the best possible environment for our students and we believe we’ve found it in Work Nicer co-working.

What we built

We want you to leave this class with something you have built yourself. Our classes focus on creating a minimum viable product using readily available web tools as well as how to run a startup. Our Calgary class used wordpress under the instruction of web development professional Myranda Wicks to prototype a website for each student based on their personal goals. We keep your project in mind in both our lecture portion of the class but also the project portion. Our Calgary students learned how to create a email campaign to collect contact info of interested consumers and embed that in their website using MailChimp.Tools like MailChimp are free to use but can make a world of difference in order to get feedback from potiental clientelle, and we want to make sure you can reep their benefits.

Our Calgary Instructors

Our Calgary class was ran by three talented instructors. Our web development expert was Myranda Wicks, she’s a SAIT educated designer with an extensive portfolio. Her work with True market can be seen at awiretech.com, the Rubix Lending Company, Play It Interactive as well as many other professional sites. Our business guest speaker Paul Lee has worked for Microsoft, Mithril Capital, and Signa Ventures as well as founded several small businesses. Dudon Wai, our dedicated business instructor, has worked with HubSpot, SVBR ‘15 and OneMonth and is himself a small business startup owner. We also had several student TAs to help student out along the way. We pick our instructors very carefully so they are relatable and able to answer your questions as well as being experts in their field.
